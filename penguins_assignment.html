Analysis of covariance (ANCOVA)

Question: Is the relationship between penguin culmen length -(response)- and culmen depth -(covariate)- significantly different between different species?

Null Hypotheses (H0):

1. The means between the species are not significantly different from each other.

2. The slopes of the regression lines between culmen length and culmen depth are not significantly different to 0.

3a. There is no relationship between penguin species and penguin culmen length.

3b. There is no relationship between penguin species and penguin culmen depth.

Assumptions:

1. Observations are independent, each subject belongs to one species only.

2. Distribution of data is approximately normally distributed.

3. The variance for the three species are approximately equal.

4. There are no significant outliers.

Although there are a some outliers on either end of the Q-Q plot. The points all fall approximately along the reference line, thus the data does not provide strong enough evidence that the data violates the approximate normality assumption.
